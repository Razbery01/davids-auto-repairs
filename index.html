About David's Auto Repairs

A workshop built on trust, skill and good old-fashioned work ethic.

David's Auto Repairs is a family-run mechanical workshop in the heart of Pietermaritzburg's CBD. We do the unglamorous, important work — keeping the cars of working families, small businesses and local fleets safe, reliable and on the road.

From a quick oil change to a complete engine rebuild, every job goes through David personally. You won't get pushed into work you don't need. You'll get a clear quote, a real diagnosis, and a vehicle that runs the way it should.

  • Fully equipped workshop with diagnostic scanners for all makes
  • Quality OEM and aftermarket parts — your choice, your budget
  • Honest, no-pressure advice from a mechanic who actually answers the phone
  • Convenient CBD location — drop and go, walk to town if needed
